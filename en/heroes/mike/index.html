Mike

SSR Melee

Mike is an SSR melee hero in Tiles Survive. He belongs to the Aeronaut faction and specializes in frontline protection with Blast Shield attacks, suppression control, survivability boosts, and defensive sustain.

At max level and 10 stars, he provides March Capacity +20.0K, Hero Attack +64.38K, Hero Defense +12.87K, and Hero Health +4.29M.

Hero Story

Mike comes from a political family. His mother is a distinguished Black congresswoman. He grew up admiring his mother's work in the community, but his faith was shattered by political corruption.

He abandoned politics and became a state trooper to confront the darkness directly, defending the vulnerable with an iron fist.

During the doomsday, Mike remains a pillar of justice, an indispensable protector in a broken world.
